Monkey D. Luffy



Monkey D. Luffy is the protagonist of the One Piece anime. He is a young pirate with rubber-like abilities gained from eating a Devil Fruit. He assembles a crew to find the legendary treasure, the One Piece, and become the Pirate King. He is known for his trademark straw hat and his unwavering determination.

Roronoa Zoro


Roronoa Zoro is a swordsman and member of Luffy's crew in the One Piece anime. He dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman and wields three swords in battle. He is known for his stern demeanor and loyalty to his friends, and has a tendency to get lost.

Marshall D. Teach


Marshall D. Teach, also known as Blackbeard, is a notorious pirate in the One Piece anime. He was once a member of Whitebeard's crew and later betrayed and killed him. He has the power to control darkness through the use of a unique Devil Fruit and seeks to become the Pirate King.
